If its a single distribution subject,enter the marks with a plus sign in between and enter the description of the distribution in the Distribution name column.
For Example:If its a theory subject the Distribution column would look like 10+30+10+50
and the DistributionName column would like Minor-1+Mid+Minor-2+End
If its a Double distribution subject(theory+lab),enter the marks of distributions by seperating it with a comma and enter marks with a plus sign in between and enter the description of the distribution in the Distribution name column by seperating them with a comma.
For Example:If its a theory+lab subject the Distribution column would look like 10+30+10+50,60+40
and the DistributionName column would like Minor-1+Mid+Minor-2+End,ContinuousAssessment+External
If there are any exams for which the student has to secure minimum number of marks, then those can be mentioned in Passing Thresholds fields.
For Example:For a theory subject(Minor-I+Mid+Minor-II+EndSem) ,if there is condition that a student has to get 17.5 marks in the end semester exam then
passing thresholds field should be filled as following 0+0+0+17.5
For Example:For a theory+lab subject(Minor-I+Mid+Minor-II+EndSem,Continuous+External)(10+30+10+50,60+40) ,if there is condition that a student has to get 17.5 marks in the end semester exam then
passing thresholds field should be filled as following 0+0+0+17.5,0+14
